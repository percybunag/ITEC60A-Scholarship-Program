ABOUT THE PROGRAM


Welcome to the City of Imus Scholarship Program, a tribute of Mayor Alex Advincula's consistent commitment to education and developing the community.

This program aims to empower the youth of Imus by giving them access to high-quality educational opportunities, all under the visionary leadership of Mayor Advincula. The scholarship program supports deserving students in following their academic goals, regardless of their background or financial condition, with a focus on diversity and merit.

Mayor Advincula partners with local stakeholders and educational institutions to make sure the initiative keeps growing and has a significant impact on the lives of Imus people. Under the unwavering direction of Mayor Alex Advincula, join us in our mission to use education to foster a better future for the City of Imus.

Mayor Alex Advincula

Mayor, City of Imus

QUALIFICATION FOR SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM:

  • Natural-born Filipino citizen residing at City of Imus, Cavite

  • Parents (Mother and Father) are registered voter of this City.

  • The applicant should be a registered voter in case of the absence of his or her parent.

  • Maintaining an average grade of 85% or 2.0 GPA.

REQUIREMENTS FOR APPLYING SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM:

  • Certificate of Grades

  • Registration Form of Current Semester

  • Photocopy of School ID with Specimen’s Signature

  • Personal Letter for Mayor Advincula

  • Barangay Clearance

  • Scholarship Form (Provided by Office of the Scholarship Program)
